Dr. Ahmed Sakr, MD

PGY-1 Psychiatry Resident at Creighton University

Welcome to my professional portfolio. I am a psychiatry resident at Creighton University with research experience in treatment-resistant depression and interventional psychiatry. My work focuses on developing individualized treatments for patients through innovative approaches including prefrontal cortical stimulation and vagal nerve stimulation.

Areas of Focus

Education & Training

I received my M.B.B.Ch. degree from Tanta University Faculty of Medicine in 2023 and am currently a PGY-1 Psychiatry Resident at Creighton University in Omaha, Nebraska.

Research

From June 2022 to June 2024, I was a Post Doctoral Research Scholar at the Interventional Psychiatry (Nahas) Research Lab at the University of Minnesota, focusing on treatment-resistant depression, exploring interventional treatments and functional MRI techniques.

Clinical Experience

My clinical interests include treatment-resistant depression and interventional psychiatry. I am currently training at CHI Health Creighton University Medical Center, gaining experience with diverse patient populations.

Professional Statement

I am dedicated to advancing the field of psychiatry through research and clinical practice, with a particular focus on developing innovative treatments for patients with treatment-resistant depression. My goal is to combine rigorous research with compassionate patient care to improve outcomes for those with complex psychiatric conditions.
